Our Story

Ousadia Tech Solutions is a forward-thinking ICT and data solutions provider, empowering organizations through technology, insight, and innovation.

Founded in 2022 by Masego Malapile, the company began its journey as a coaching and training business focused on personal and professional growth. Over time, as the digital landscape evolved, Ousadia expanded its focus — transforming into a full-fledged ICT partner that bridges the gap between technology adoption and human capability.

Today, Ousadia Tech Solutions specializes in delivering integrated digital transformation services, including business intelligence consulting, data analytics and visualization, software development and training, software and infrastructure solutions, and ICT skills development.

We combine technical expertise with human-centered training and coaching, ensuring that technology implementation leads to real, measurable business outcomes. Our approach goes beyond deploying systems — we help organizations use data intelligently, modernize their processes, and upskill their people to thrive in a digital economy.

Our History & Evolution

From coaching and training to a full-service ICT solutions provider

2022

Founding Year

Founded by Masego Malapile as a coaching and soft skills training company focused on personal and professional growth.

2023

Expansion

Expanded into organizational training, combining coaching with business development strategies.

2024

ICT Transformation

Transitioned into a full-service ICT solutions provider, focusing on business intelligence, data analytics, and ICT consulting, while retaining the coaching and training arm to ensure holistic development.

2025

Digital Transformation Partner

Evolved into a trusted digital transformation and data intelligence partner, expanding Power BI and analytics services, launching Microsoft-aligned training, and offering managed BI and advisory solutions that connect technology, people, and performance.

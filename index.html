Ousadia Tech Solutions

A forward-thinking ICT and data solutions provider empowering organizations across Africa

Founded in 2022 by Masego Malapile, Ousadia Tech Solutions began as a coaching and training business focused on personal and professional growth. As the digital landscape evolved, we expanded our focus to become a full-fledged ICT partner.

Today, we specialize in delivering integrated digital transformation services, including business intelligence consulting, data analytics and visualization, software development, and ICT skills development.

We combine technical expertise with human-centered training and coaching, ensuring that technology implementation leads to real, measurable business outcomes.
